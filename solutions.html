Our solutions

Timeline Management

Run-of-Show

Build your complete event cue list inside Cenika. Attach technical riders, crew assignments and equipment lists to every cue. As the show progresses, a live dashboard advances automatically and flags any delays before they cascade.

  • Drag-and-drop cue builder
  • Live countdown timers per cue
  • Multi-department visibility
  • Import from Excel or CSV

People & Roles

Crew Board

Assign every crew member a role with clear responsibilities. Briefing packs are distributed in-app. Each person acknowledges their assignment — you see confirmation on a single board without chasing anyone down by radio.

  • Role-based access and visibility
  • In-app acknowledgements
  • QR-code crew onboarding
  • Department-level dashboards

Technical Readiness

Tech Sign-off

Every technical domain — audio, lighting, LED, RF spectrum, and broadcast — has a dedicated sign-off checklist. Department heads verify and sign. No cue block can advance to "ready" until all relevant checks are green.

  • Domain-specific checklists
  • Named sign-off accountability
  • Block-advance gate logic
  • Audit trail for every check

Emergency Response

Incident Mode

When something goes wrong — and in live events it always does — Incident Mode gives your team a structured, calm response. Log the incident, assign resolution ownership, escalate if needed, and close with a documented record.

  • One-tap incident activation
  • Severity classification
  • Escalation chain management
  • Post-incident report export
