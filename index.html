How It Works

One System. Zero Gaps.

Cenika connects every moving part of your event production in a single operational view.

  1. 01

    Build Your Show

    Import your runsheet or build cue-by-cue inside Cenika. Attach tasks, technical requirements, and responsible crew to each cue.

  2. 02

    Distribute role assignments and briefing packs. Crew acknowledge their duties in-app — you see confirmation in real time.

  3. 03

    Debrief & Improve
