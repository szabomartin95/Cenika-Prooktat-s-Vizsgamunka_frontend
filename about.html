Our Story

Built by People Who've Run Shows

Cenika was born from years of managing stadium-scale events with clipboards, WhatsApp groups, and spreadsheets. We knew there had to be a better way.

Mission

Why We Built Cenika

Live events are one of the last great logistical challenges that haven't been solved by software. Every other industry — aviation, construction, healthcare — has operational tools built specifically for its complexity. Live entertainment hadn't.

We spent years as production managers, technical directors, and stage managers at some of the world's biggest arenas. We saw the same problems every night: miscommunicated cues, unsigned-off checks, unclear escalation paths. Cenika fixes all of it.

What Drives Us

Our Values

  • Principle 1

    Reliability First

    A show cannot wait for a loading spinner. Every feature we build is stress-tested against the worst venue infrastructure imaginable.

  • Principle 2

    Designed for Stress

    Show day is not the time for a learning curve. Cenika's UX is built for tunnel-vision focus — the right action is always one tap away.

  • Principle 3

    People-Centred

    The crew is not a resource to be scheduled. Cenika treats every person on the team as a professional who deserves clear information and respect.

  • Principle 4

    Continuous Learning

    Every incident log, every debrief, every close call becomes structured knowledge. Shows get smarter every time you use Cenika.
